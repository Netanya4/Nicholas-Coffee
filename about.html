This is Us

Nicholas' coffee was set up all for the love of coffee and pastries . Working in the oilfield really makes on wake up to drinking coffee, walk around and drinking coffee and having a cup of coffee over discussions and meetings. This just sticks because once you get to savour the sweet smell of coffee and your buds feel the lovely taste of coffee, there is no way back because you just have to keep going back to one of the gifts of nature to mankind. So what did we do? We set up Nicholas' Coffee so that we get to enjoy everyday and also share with the world the beauty of coffee.

-Nicholas Chidubem
 Founder.
